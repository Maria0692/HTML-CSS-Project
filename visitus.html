Museum Information

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired. For guests with hearing impairments, the museum provides real-time captioning and American Sign Language interpretation during all guided tours and educational programs. Induction loop systems are installed throughout the galleries to enhance the audio experience for hearing aid users.The museum also has a designated quiet room where visitors can take breaks from the stimulation of the galleries. This space features comfortable seating, soft lighting, and noise-cancelling headphones available for use. Staff are trained to recognize signs of sensory overload and can direct guests to this calming retreat if needed.

Food and drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more. The café sources many of its ingredients locally and sustainably, supporting regional farmers and producers. They have prominent labeling to highlight which menu items are organic, fair trade, or made with recycled/recyclable packaging.For visitors who may need to bring their own food, the café has a designated seating area with microwave ovens and refrigerators available for reheating or storing personal meals. There are also ample tables and comfortable lounge seating throughout the café space.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum. The shop's inventory is carefully curated to complement the museum's exhibits and programming. Many of the products are exclusive collaborations with the museum, allowing visitors to take home unique items they can't find elsewhere.For younger guests, there are interactive play stations set up in the shop where children can test out some of the science gadgets and games before purchasing. This helps ensure families find the right fit for their interests and skill levels.
